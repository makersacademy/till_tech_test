User Stories for Till Tech Test

* As a customer
  So that I have a sense of what I bought
  I would like to see a receipt with a list of items

* As a money-conscious customer
  So that I know how much each item cost
  I would like to see a line total

* As a customer
  So that I know how much to pay
  I would like to have the total cost calculated for me

* As a law-abiding citizen
  So that I fund the govt
  I would like to pay tax

* As a money-conscious customer
  So I can take what is mine
  I would like to receive change from the till

* As a customer looking to capitalize on promotions
  So that I can achieve greater satisfaction from my purchase
  I would like to be able to receive the discounts that the store offers
